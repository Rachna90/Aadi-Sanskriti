Aadi Sanskriti

Welcome to the Aadi Sanskriti platform, which promotes learning and engagement with rich and diverse tribal socio-cultural heritages and life. Our mission is to empower tribal communities through the preservation and promotion of their traditions while fostering their socio-economic development. Through Aadi Sanskriti, immerse yourself in the world of Aadi Viswavidyalaya, our tribally centered online learning hub, where you can learn the intricacies of their arts and crafts from expert tribal artists and artisans. Also get driven into Aadi Sampada, a rich repository of tribal heritage, livelihoods, traditions, and indigenous knowledge, a vibrant testimony to the deep-rooted cultural heritage of the tribal peoples.

Enter into the wonderful world of tribes, learn about them, and learn from them.

Aadi Vishwavidyalaya

Aadi Vishwavidyalaya is an innovative e-learning platform focused on exploring, preserving, and promoting India's tribal heritage. It engages the learners with diverse traditions and art forms of the tribal communities, fostering appreciation and awareness. Learn from our two comprehensive courses – Beginner and Advance - around tribal life, art, and cultures. Whether you are a novice or experienced in this subject, Aadi Vishwavidyalaya provides structured and enriching learning. All courses are designed and guided by experts, including tribal artists and scholars, thus giving learners an authentic and insightful experience. Engage with us to explore, learn, and celebrate the living traditions of India's tribal communities.

Aadi Sampada

This digital repository is a unique one stop destination to explore and celebrate India's vast tribal heritage. The platform presents rich collection of knowledge, resources, and insights about diverse traditions, artistic expressions, and cultural practices of tribal communities across the country.

From traditional art forms and indigenous crafts to folklore, rituals, and ways of life, Aadi Sampada seeks to preserve and promote all the priceless contributions of the tribal society. It serves as a link between age-old traditions and the current trends so that the wisdom and preciousness the communities had to offer can be accessible for generations to come.

Immerse yourself in the beauty, resilience, and knowledge of India's tribal cultures with Aadi Sampada - your gateway to the timeless treasures of indigenous traditions.

Aadi Haat

Empowering Tribal Artisans Digitally

Aadi Haat is revolutionizing the way tribal artisans interact with the modern market by providing a digital platform for them to sell their art and handicrafts. In the digital age, this marketplace helps connect these artisans to a global audience, allowing their unique craftsmanship to be appreciated far and wide. It also provides them with the opportunity to set fair prices for their creations, ensuring they receive equitable compensation for their hard work. This digital shift is a crucial step in empowering tribal communities, fostering a sense of independence and pride in their craft.
